About Me

I'm Fatima Raadia, a Software Engineer and PhD researcher based in Michigan, USA.

I specialize in Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), real-time embedded systems, and algorithm development. With hands-on experience in C++, Python, and MQTT (Mosquitto), I design real-time communication systems, camera streaming, and data visualization tools. My focus is on networked systems, software optimization, security protocols, and delivering innovative solutions through collaborative problem-solving.

I enjoy exploring cutting-edge technologies, mentoring others, and sharing knowledge to foster innovation in software engineering and research.
